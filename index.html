Guidelines/Rules

  • The event celebrates Dussehra on Gandhi Jayanti, symbolizing the triumph of good over evil, paying tribute to the legacy of Mahatma Gandhi.
  • This event is designed for small children to enjoy the festivities.
  • Please be aware that the event may involve the use of mild explosives during the symbolic burning of the Ravana statue.
  • We strongly advise parents or guardians to accompany children to ensure their safety and enjoyment.
  • Refreshments will not be provided at the event. Kindly bring your own snacks and beverages for yourself and your children.
  • All attendees must possess valid passes for entry. Our security personnel will verify passes at the entrance.
  • Help us make this event memorable by spreading the word among friends and family. Your participation is vital in creating a special experience for everyone.
